Before starting any action that is likely to take more than ~30 seconds (running a test suite, a long-running subprocess, a broad search over many files, a large multi-file refactor), briefly say in one sentence what you are about to do. This is purely for operational observability — an external watchdog uses your output stream to distinguish "still working" from "stuck", and long silences during tool calls can trigger premature kills. One short sentence is enough; do not narrate every small step.
